🏛️About HACK

HACK is a student-led club at KUET focused on hardware: PCBs, microcontrollers, sensors, robotics basics, and hands-on electronics workshops. We learn by soldering, wiring, debugging, and shipping small projects together.

🎯Who it is for

Anyone at KUET curious about physical tech — not only EEE majors. If you want LEDs to blink for a reason you understand, you belong here.
